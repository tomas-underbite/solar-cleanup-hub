The team.

Cape Town-based. Built for commercial solar. Focused on the gap between commissioning and performance.

Kleen Up Solar was founded on a straightforward observation: South Africa is commissioning solar assets faster than anyone is maintaining them. Most portfolios are degrading quietly. We built the capability to fix that: IEC-compliant thermal drone inspections, professional panel cleaning, and the operational track record to back it up. 70 sites. 80,000+ panels. 120 MW under management.

“South Africa is building solar faster than anyone is maintaining it. We built Kleen Up Solar to change that.”

Vision

To be South Africa’s most trusted solar O&M partner, the first call for every asset owner who wants their portfolio performing at full capacity.

Mission

We protect the performance of commercial solar assets through disciplined, IEC-compliant thermal inspections and professional panel cleaning.

Values

Flat-rate quotes locked in before we fly. IEC TS 62446-3:2017 compliance on every site without exception. And a straightforward preference for long-term partnerships over once-off jobs.

Bradley Eachus

Company Owner

Bradley spent a decade in solar across South Africa, Canada, and international markets before founding Kleen Up Solar in 2024. As Director of Business Development at Solar Earth Technologies for five years, and prior to that in the same role at Ergosun Integrated Solar Roof Tiles in Ontario, he worked across pricing, strategic partnerships, and market entry for solar companies in multiple countries. He was selected as a speaker at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory’s Beyond The Panel Conference in Denver.

What that experience made clear was a structural gap in the market: assets were being commissioned and then left to degrade, with owners relying on rain and annual checkups rather than systematic monitoring. Performance losses of 10 to 40 percent were common and largely invisible without proper inspection. Kleen Up Solar was built to close that gap.

Bradley holds a BA in Socio-Informatics from Stellenbosch University and is a native Afrikaans and English speaker.

Gareth Hayden

Director

Gareth is the Director of Kleen Up, the commercial services company that Kleen Up Solar grew out of. He studied Business Science (Accounting and Finance) at the University of Cape Town and spent over a decade as a partner at South Seas Import Export before launching Kleen Up in 2016, building it into an established Cape Town operation.

The move into solar O&M was a natural extension. South Africa’s commercial solar sector was expanding rapidly, but the maintenance infrastructure was not keeping pace. Gareth recognised that a disciplined, operationally focused business was exactly what the market needed, and that Kleen Up already had the systems, the teams, and the standards to do it properly.

As Director of Kleen Up Solar, Gareth oversees operations across the company’s growing portfolio of commercial solar clients, ensuring every inspection and every clean meets the standards the business was built on.
