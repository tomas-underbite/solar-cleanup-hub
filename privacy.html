Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Kleen Up Solar (Pty) Ltd, Cape Town, South Africa

Kleen Up Solar is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how long we retain it, and what rights you have.

1. What we collect

When you submit an enquiry or quote request through our website, we collect:

  • Full name and company name
  • Email address and telephone number
  • Site address and number of panels (where provided)
  • Any other information you choose to include in your message

We do not collect sensitive personal information, and we do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Standard web server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) are retained for security and analytics purposes in accordance with our Google Analytics configuration.

2. Purpose of collection

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • Schedule and deliver the service you have requested
  • Send you service-related communications (e.g., inspection date confirmation, report delivery)

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your explicit consent.

3. Retention

We retain your personal information for 24 months from the date of collection, or for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, whichever is longer. After this period, your information is securely deleted. If you become an active client, we retain information for the duration of the commercial relationship plus 24 months.

4. Your rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your information, subject to any legal retention obligations
  • Object to the processing of your information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@kleenup.solar. We will respond within 10 business days.

5. Complaints

If you believe your personal information has been processed unlawfully or your rights have not been respected, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa:

  • Website: www.inforegulator.org.za
  • Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za
  • Phone: 012 406 4818

6. Contact

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at:

  • Email: info@kleenup.solar
  • Website: kleenup.solar/contact
